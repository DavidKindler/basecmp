  • Icons

    A series of icons has been developed to support and enhance our communication tools. Their use is at the discretion of the designer, and there is no maximum or minimum number that should be included in any given document. If you decide to add icons to your document, please ensure APPROPRIATENESS – i.e. if your document concerns Automotive, choose an automotive-related icon.

    • White only
