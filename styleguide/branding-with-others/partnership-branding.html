Partnership branding

The ‘Partner of NXP’ logo is a special use version of our standard NXP logo and as such, many of the same guidelines apply for its usage. You must never change it in any way, under any circumstances. This ensures legal protection and helps recognition and awareness worldwide.

Why do we use ‘Partner of NXP’ logos?

‘Partner of NXP’ logos are used by distributors and other business partners to demonstrate their strong affiliation with NXP. For more information on partner programs, visit http://www.nxp.com/about/partner-program.html

‘Partner of NXP’ logo versions

As with the standard NXP logo, we have created the ‘Partner of NXP’ logo in different versions:

1.  FULL-COLOR LOGO: This default logo should be used on virtually all materials
2.  BLACK-AND-WHITE LOGO: For use in newsprint or on a fax sheet

‘Partner of NXP’ logo colors

As with the standard NXP logo, the colors for the ‘Partner of NXP’ logo have been specially chosen to be dynamic and inspiring.

A holding shape has been designed to contain the ingredient line. Another important function of the holding shape is to provide a protected area for NXP.

The correct colors are specified below for use in pixel and print – CMYK (full-color for print) and RGB (for digital).

Minimum size

Our logo must always be shown in a clear and legible form. To help ensure this, please adhere to the minimum size requirements shown below for print and on-screen use.

Minimum clear space

Our logo must always be shown in a clear and legible form. To help ensure this, please always maintain a ‘clear space’ around the logo – where no text, images or symbols may appear.

The easy-to-follow rule to achieve the correct clear space is to ensure a minimum space of 50% of the height of the logo is always left clear around the ‘Partner of NXP’ logo (see example).

Never...

Consistency is crucial for our brand identity to be effective. Nowhere is that more important than when using the ‘Partner of NXP’ logo. You must ensure the ‘Partner of NXP’ logo is never altered or tampered with. Here are some examples of the ways that the ‘Partner of NXP’ logo could be compromised.

Applications

The ‘Partner of NXP’ logo is used to demonstrate a distributor’s and business partner’s strong affiliation with NXP. Here are some guidelines on its use:

  • When shown with other partner logos or badges, the ‘Partner of NXP’ logo should always be the dominant brand
  • The ‘Partner of NXP’ logo should appear in full-color whenever possible
  • The ‘Partner of NXP’ logo should not overshadow the distributor logo
