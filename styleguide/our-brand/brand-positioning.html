Our brand
positioning

  • Secure connections for a smarter world

    Four mega trends are reshaping the way we live. Energy efficiency, connected devices, security and health are the global game-changers triggering radical societal transformation at a speed unprecedented in modern history.
    NXP Semiconductors designs and manufactures High-Performance Mixed-Signal Solutions that enable the Secure Connections needed for this smarter world.

    Solutions for 'engineers of innovation'

    To meet the demands of a fast-paced, super-connected, landscape, the electronics industry faces unparalleled time-to-market pressures.
    Whether you're building solutions for the automotive, industrial, consumer, lighting or identification markets, the ability to thrive comes from having access to innovative solutions in the areas of RF, Analog, Power and Digital Processing.

    NXP's solutions are at the heart of:
    • Security - making devices, data and people connect securely and safely
    • Connectivity - ensuring that devices and systems operating across multiple standards can talk to each other seamlessly
    • Power - making the connected world energy efficient
    • Health - improving the capacity of health systems to provide quality care
    • NXP Semiconductors is built for you: the engineers of innovation.
  • Our heritage

    Founded more than 50 years ago as Philips Semiconductors, NXP is built on rich technology heritage.
    Building on its expertise in High Performance Mixed Signal electronics, NXP provides the technologies to power the automotive, identification and mobile industries, in application areas including wireless infrastructure, lighting, healthcare, industrial, consumer technology and computing.
    NXP's solutions deliver greater energy efficiency, safety, security and performance. Combined with application insights, sub-system expertise and standard products, we can provide multiple solutions to engineering problems.
    Today, the NXP brand stands for the innovation and versatility that design engineers need and value in today's constantly changing markets. At NXP, we can support you from vision to concept to manufacturing - helping you deliver smarter, secure, connections.

    Investing for the future

    NXP is committed to innovate for the benefit of our customers, our communities and society overall. Our research and development is focused on areas that contribute to improving quality of life for all, driving a smarter way of living.
    The technology that underpins the brave new connected world is often invisible to consumers - but it's what's behind the scenes that really matters. It's vital that we continue to develop our solutions to make connections as secure, seamless and energy-efficient as possible.
    And we extend this commitment to everything we do, through our corporate social responsibility programs.
