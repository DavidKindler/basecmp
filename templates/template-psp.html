The Kinetis K2x MCU family includes 512KB-1MB of flash memory, a single precision floating point unit, full and high-speed USB 2.0 On-The-Go with device charge detect, and a NAND flash controller. The Kinetis K2x family is available in 144 LQFP and 144 MAPBGA packages.

Features
Ultra-Low-Power
  • 10 low-power modules with power and clock gating for optimal peripheral activity and recovery times
  • Full memory and analog operation down to 1.71 volts for extended battery life.
  • Low leaking wake-up unit with p to eight internal modules and 16 piun as wake-up sources.
Flash, SRAM and FlexMemory
  • 512KB - 1MB Flash, fast access, high reliability with four level security protection.
  • FlexMemory: 32 bytes-16KB of user-segmentable byte write/erase EEPROM.
  • EEPROM with over 10M cycles and flash with 70 usec write time.

Mixed-Signal capability, High Performance and improved timing and control features Learn More >
